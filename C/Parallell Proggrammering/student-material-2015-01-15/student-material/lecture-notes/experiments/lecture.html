Lecture 11: Experimentation and presentation

Objectives

After actively participating in this lecture you should be able to

  1. Design experiments to evaluate a parallel program's performance.
  2. Evaluate an experiment design.
  3. Summarize and present results obtained from experiments.

Pre-lecture

Before attending this lecture, you should do the following:

  1. Read about speedup and efficiency in your book.
