Exercise: Termination detection

Prerequisites

Before you start, you should do the following:

  1. Attend the lecture on load balancing and termination detection.

Objectives

After completing this exercise you should be able to

  1. Define the termination detection problem.
  2. Describe and explain a termination detection algorithm.

Instructions

Download and carefully study the classic article "Derivation of a termination detection algorithm for distributed computations" (see the Resources section).
